About the University



HEC has granted NOC via lett er No. 16-64/HEC/A&A/2010/401 to Abasyn University to open its campus at Islamabad. The University has established state of art faciliti es in Sector I-9/2 for the campus. Highly qualifi ed teaching and non teaching staff have been hired. The campus has also established fully equipped labs with latest state of the art technologies and tools. The campus has also developed a well stocked library which has access to digital research databases, e-journals, e-books and e-reports.
